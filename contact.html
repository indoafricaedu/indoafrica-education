Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is the student visa guaranteed?

A: Yes. When a student qualifies for admission and submits complete and genuine documents as guided by our team, the Indian student visa is approved in line with embassy regulations. Our team assists students in preparing correct documentation to ensure successful outcomes.

Q: Do I have to pay for your services?

A: No. We provide free guidance and application support to all students. Our service is covered by partnerships with universities.

Q: What's the difference between universities with and without flight tickets?

A: Some universities offer a first flight ticket to India as part of their international student package, while others do not. All other admission processes and visa requirements remain the same.

Q: How long does the application process take?

A: From application submission to receiving an offer letter typically takes 2-4 weeks. The complete process including visa approval usually takes 6-8 weeks.

Q: What documents do I need to apply?

A: You will need: passport copy, academic certificates (O-Level, A-Level, or Diploma), academic transcripts, and a CV/Resume. Additional documents may be required depending on the specific university.
